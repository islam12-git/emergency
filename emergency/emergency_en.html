Tourist Emergency Instructions

Sudden Health Emergency (Fainting – Shock – Allergy – Injury)

  1. Ensure the place is safe for the victim.
  2. Call the ambulance immediately (local emergency number).
  3. Do not give any medication unless you know the person takes it.
  4. If fainting: raise the legs slightly and loosen tight clothes.
  5. If bleeding: apply direct pressure with a clean cloth.
  6. Inform the tour guide or trip manager immediately.
  7. Keep a copy of passport and health insurance.

Fire in Hotel or Transportation

  1. Activate the fire alarm immediately.
  2. Use stairs only; do not use the elevator.
  3. Cover your nose with a wet cloth.
  4. Move low to avoid smoke inhalation.
  5. Gather at the agreed evacuation point.
  6. Count people to ensure everyone is out safely.

Lost Passport or Belongings

  1. Report to local police immediately and file a report.
  2. Contact your embassy to get a replacement travel document.
  3. Contact the airline if you have a nearby flight.
  4. Keep an electronic copy of your passport for easier processing.

Missing Tourist from the Group

  1. Designate a clear meeting point in advance.
  2. Try to contact the person immediately.
  3. Wait at the agreed meeting point.
  4. Inform security or management after 15–20 minutes.
  5. Always keep the guide's number.

Traffic Accident During the Trip

  1. Do not leave the scene unless in direct danger.
  2. Call ambulance and police.
  3. Provide first aid to victims until emergency services arrive.
  4. Document the accident with photos if possible.
  5. Inform the travel company immediately.

Natural Disasters (Flood – Earthquake – Storm)

  1. Earthquake: Take cover under a strong table, stay away from glass, avoid elevators, exit after shaking stops.
  2. Storm or Flood: Stay in a safe place, away from windows, avoid driving in accumulated water.

Theft or Assault

  1. Do not resist to maintain safety.
  2. Memorize the assailant's features if possible.
  3. Move to a safe place immediately.
  4. Report to police and embassy.
  5. Stop bank cards immediately.
  6. Preventive tips before any trip: take passport photo, know local emergency numbers, hotel address, carry a local SIM or internet package, inform a relative of travel plan.

Terrorist Attack (Shooting – Explosion – Hostage)

  1. Shooting: Escape immediately if possible; if not, hide in a closed place and lock the door, silence phone, avoid windows, do not record the event, follow security instructions.
  2. Explosion: Move away immediately, avoid crowds, check yourself and others for injuries, call ambulance.
  3. Hostage Situation: Stay calm, avoid direct eye contact, follow instructions without argument, memorize details quietly.

War or Armed Conflict in the Country

  1. Stay inside the hotel or a safe place.
  2. Follow embassy updates for your country.
  3. Prepare a small emergency bag (passport, cash, phone, charger, basic meds).
  4. Stay away from windows and open spaces.
  5. Do not go out during curfew.
  6. Contact travel company for evacuation arrangements if needed.
  7. Register your name at the embassy for easier evacuation.
  8. Suggested emergency bag: passport + copy, cash, phone + power bank, basic medications, small water bottle, small flashlight.
